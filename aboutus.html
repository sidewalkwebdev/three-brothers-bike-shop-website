THE LEGACY

It runs in the family.

We are 3 sons who want to keep the spirit of biking alive.

Our story starts back in 1985. Our father ran a bike shop, teaching us everything he knew about mechanics, customer service, and the joy of two wheels.

Today, we are three brothers who not only fix bikes but ride them every day. We took over the wrench to honor that legacy. Whether you need a simple tube change or a complex restoration, we treat every bike like it's our own.
